Baked Brisket

Fresh Baked beef brisket

Beef Brisket is an excellent choice for all of you BBQ lovers!

This recipe will satisfy your wildest desires. Try it!

Ingredients

  • First-cut brisket
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • Butterr
  • Large sliced yellow onion
  • Minced fresh rosemary
  • Apple juice

Steps

  1. Season brisket
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  3. Melt butter, add onion, salt, garlic, rosemary and apple juice
  4. Put half of mixture into baking dish, place brisket on top, cover with remaining mixture
  5. Bake in oven for 1 hour 30 minutes
  6. Transfer brisket to a plate and tent with foil
  7. Pour onions and braising liquid into cup
  8. Slice brisket across the grain and serve with the gravy
